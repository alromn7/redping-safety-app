# REDP!NG SOS/SAR Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 19, 2026  
Last Updated: February 19, 2026  
Version: 2.1

## 1) Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to REDP!NG SOS/SAR mobile applications and related services, including Android and iOS builds, and connected cloud services used to deliver safety and emergency coordination functionality.

REDP!NG (we, us, our) provides features such as SOS activation, emergency contact alerting, location sharing, SAR coordination workflows, account management, and subscription features.

## 2) Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account and Profile Data
- Name, email address, phone number, and account identifiers
- Account settings and preferences
- Emergency contacts configured by the user
- Optional emergency profile details entered by the user (for example medical notes)

### 2.2 Location Data
- Precise location (GPS) when enabled by the user and device permissions
- Approximate location when only coarse permission is granted
- Location updates during active safety and emergency workflows
- Location context needed for maps, hazard context, and rescue coordination

### 2.3 Device, Sensor, and Diagnostic Data
- Device model, OS version, app version
- Motion and sensor signals used for safety detection workflows
- App diagnostics such as crash/error and connectivity metadata

### 2.4 Emergency and Communication Data
- SOS/session lifecycle records (activation, status, resolution)
- Emergency notification metadata and response status
- In-app emergency/SAR coordination message metadata

### 2.5 Subscription and Billing Data
- Subscription plan, entitlement state, and billing status metadata
- Payment transaction metadata required for subscription management

Payment card details are processed by payment providers/platform billing flows. REDP!NG does not store full raw card numbers.

## 3) How We Use Information

We process information to:
- Provide SOS/SAR safety and emergency coordination functions
- Alert configured emergency contacts and support responder workflows
- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Deliver and manage subscription-entitled features
- Maintain reliability, performance, and operational security
- Meet legal, regulatory, and incident-response obligations

## 4) Legal Bases for Processing

Where applicable (for example GDPR/UK GDPR), we rely on one or more legal bases:
- Performance of a contract (providing requested app services)
- Legitimate interests (service security, fraud prevention, and reliability)
- Consent (where required by law or platform policy)
- Compliance with legal obligations

## 5) How and When We Share Data

REDP!NG does not sell personal data.

We may share data:

### 5.1 Emergency Sharing
- With user-configured emergency contacts when SOS/safety workflows are triggered
- With authorized SAR/responder coordination workflows for active incidents

### 5.2 Service Providers
- With processors/vendors supporting infrastructure, notifications, messaging, analytics, billing, and customer support

### 5.3 Legal and Safety Requirements
- When required by law, legal process, or to protect users/public safety

### 5.4 Corporate Transactions
- In merger/acquisition/asset-transfer scenarios, subject to applicable legal safeguards

## 6) Data Retention

We retain data only as long as required for service operation, safety, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and legitimate business purposes.

Typical retention windows:
- Emergency/SOS operational records: up to 7 years
- Location/communication operational records: up to 2 years unless a longer period is legally required
- Account/profile data: until account deletion and completion of legal/operational retention obligations

Retention periods may vary by jurisdiction and lawful requests.

## 7) Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational safeguards, including:
- Encryption in transit
- Access controls and least-privilege controls
- Monitoring and logging for security and operational integrity

No system can guarantee absolute security, but we continuously maintain and improve safeguards.

## 8) International Data Transfers

Data may be processed in jurisdictions other than the user’s country. Where required, appropriate transfer safeguards are applied under applicable law.

## 9) User Rights and Choices

Depending on applicable law, users may request:
- Access to personal data
- Correction of inaccurate data
- Deletion of eligible data
- Data export/portability where applicable
- Restriction or objection to certain processing where legally available

Users may also manage app/device permissions (for example location and notifications) in app and device settings.

To submit a request, contact us using Section 13.

## 10) Children and Age

REDP!NG is intended for users age 13 and older. Where required by local law, users below the age of majority should use REDP!NG with parent/guardian consent and oversight.

## 14) Important Emergency Disclaimer

During active emergency workflows, REDP!NG may automatically share relevant safety data (including location and emergency profile context) with configured emergency contacts and responder coordination workflows to support incident response.

REDP!NG is a safety coordination tool and does not universally guarantee automatic contact with official emergency numbers (for example 911/112/999) in all jurisdictions, network conditions, or device states. If immediate emergency assistance is required, contact local emergency services directly whenever possible.
