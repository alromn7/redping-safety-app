### Safety-Critical Features (Always Free)
These features are **NEVER restricted** for safety/ethical reasons:
- Manual SOS activation
- Emergency location sharing during active SOS
- Read-only community safety alerts
- Basic emergency contact notification (1 contact minimum)

### AI Safety Features
- **Free**: No AI features (safety limitation)
- **Essential**: AI verification only (for crash/fall detection)
- **Essential+**: Basic AI assistant (limited command set)
- **Pro**: Full AI assistant (24 commands)
- **Ultra**: Enterprise AI (organization insights)
- **Family**: Full AI assistant with family context

### SAR Access Levels
- **Free/Essential/Essential+**: Observer mode (view alerts, cannot respond)
- **Pro**: Volunteer participation (respond to missions)
- **Ultra**: Organization management (create teams, manage members)

### Satellite Communication
- **Free/Essential**: Not available
- **Essential+**: 5 messages/month (emergency backup)
- **Pro**: 100 messages/month (regular use)
- **Ultra**: Unlimited (professional use)
- **Family**: 200 messages/month (shared pool)
