# SAR Member & Organization Registration Guide

## Overview
The SAR (Search and Rescue) registration functionality allows users to register as SAR members or register their organizations within the RedPing app. This enables emergency responders to receive and respond to emergency alerts.

## Accessing SAR Registration

### Option 1: Through Profile Page
1. Open the RedPing app
2. Navigate to the **Profile** tab (bottom navigation)
3. Scroll down to the **"SAR Services"** section
4. Choose from:
   - **SAR Member Registration** - Register as an individual SAR member
   - **Organization Registration** - Register your SAR organization
   - **Organization Dashboard** - Manage existing SAR organization
   - **SAR Verification** - Admin verification for SAR members
   - **SOS Ping Dashboard** - Monitor emergency pings (for SAR members)

### Option 2: Direct Navigation
- SAR Member Registration: Navigate to `/sar-registration`
- Organization Registration: Navigate to `/organization-registration`
- SAR Verification: Navigate to `/sar-verification`
- Organization Dashboard: Navigate to `/organization-dashboard`
- SOS Ping Dashboard: Navigate to `/sos-ping-dashboard`

## SAR Member Registration Process

### 1. Personal Information
- Full Name
- Phone Number
- Email Address
- Date of Birth
- Address Information
- Emergency Contact

### 2. Member Type Selection
- **Professional**: Paid SAR professional
- **Volunteer**: Volunteer SAR member
- **Trainee**: SAR member in training
- **Specialist**: Specialized SAR role

### 3. Credentials Upload
Required credentials may include:
- Driver's License
- Government ID
- Professional License
- Background Check
- Medical Certification
- Other relevant credentials

### 4. Certifications
Upload certifications such as:
- Wilderness First Aid
- CPR Certification
- Rescue Technician
- Mountain Rescue
- Water Rescue
- Technical Rescue
- Medical Training
- Radio Operator

### 5. Experience Information
- Years of Experience
- Number of Previous Missions
- Specializations
- Previous Organizations
- Equipment Proficiency
- Terrain Experience

## Organization Registration Process

### 1. Organization Information
- Organization Name
- Organization Type (e.g., Volunteer Non-profit, Professional, Government)
- Description
- Website
- Founded Year
- Estimated Members

### 2. Legal Information
- Legal Status (e.g., 501(c)(3) Non-profit)
- Legal Name
- Registration Number
- Tax ID
- Registration Date

### 3. Contact Information
- Primary Phone
- Email Address
- Address Information
- Contact Person Details

### 4. Capabilities
- Service Areas
- Specializations
- Equipment Available
- Vehicles
- 24/7 Availability
- Maximum Deployment Size
- Average Response Time

### 5. Organization Credentials
Upload relevant organizational documents:
- Certificate of Incorporation
- Tax Exemption Documents
- Insurance Certificates
- Licenses and Permits

## Verification Process

### For SAR Members
1. Submit registration with all required information
2. Upload credentials and certifications
3. Wait for admin verification
4. Receive notification of verification status

### For Organizations
1. Complete organization registration
2. Upload all required documentation
3. Admin review process
4. Verification and activation

## Using SAR Services

### SOS Ping Dashboard
Once verified as a SAR member, access the SOS Ping Dashboard to:
- Monitor real-time emergency pings
- Filter by priority and location
- Respond to emergency situations
- Communicate with civilians in distress
- Update response status

### Organization Dashboard
Organization admins can:
- Manage team members
- Track operations
- View performance metrics
- Coordinate response efforts
- Manage organizational settings

## Features Available to SAR Members

1. **Real-time Emergency Monitoring**: Receive emergency pings in real-time
2. **Cross-Emulator Communication**: Communicate across different devices
3. **Location-based Response**: Filter emergencies by proximity
4. **Priority Management**: Handle high-priority emergencies first
5. **Response Tracking**: Track response times and success rates
6. **Team Coordination**: Coordinate with other SAR members
7. **Civilian Communication**: Direct messaging with civilians in distress
