# REDP!NG Safety App - Usage Policies

**Last Updated: December 20, 2024**  
**Version: 1.0**

## 1. GENERAL USAGE GUIDELINES

### 1.1 Intended Use
REDP!NG is designed for:
- **Emergency Response**: Personal safety and emergency situation management
- **Activity Safety**: Safety monitoring during outdoor activities and adventures
- **Community Safety**: Local community safety coordination and communication
- **Professional SAR**: Search and rescue operations by qualified teams
- **Help and Support**: Non-emergency assistance and support coordination

### 1.2 Prohibited Uses
REDP!NG must NOT be used for:
- **Illegal Activities**: Any illegal or unauthorized activities
- **False Emergencies**: Intentionally triggering false emergency responses
- **Harassment**: Harassing, threatening, or stalking other users
- **Spam**: Sending unsolicited messages or communications
- **Commercial Exploitation**: Unauthorized commercial use of app features
- **Data Mining**: Unauthorized collection or harvesting of user data
- **System Interference**: Attempting to interfere with app functionality
