## 1. Information We Collect

We collect several types of information to provide and improve the App:

### 1.1 Personal Information

**"Personal Information"** means information that identifies you as an individual or relates to an identifiable individual.

We collect the following Personal Information:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Identity Information** | Name, date of birth, gender |
| **Contact Information** | Email address, phone number, mailing address |
| **Account Credentials** | Username, password (encrypted), security questions |
| **Payment Information** | Credit/debit card details, billing address (processed by third-party payment processors) |
| **Professional Credentials** (Physician Users) | Medical license number, NPI (National Provider Identifier), DEA number, malpractice insurance details |

### 1.2 Health Information

**"Health Information"** or **"PHI" (Protected Health Information)** means information about your health status, medical history, or healthcare services.

We collect the following Health Information:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Vital Signs** | Blood pressure, heart rate, weight, body temperature, blood glucose |
| **Health Metrics** | BMI, Health Index score, fitness assessment results |
| **Medical History** | Conditions, diagnoses (user-entered), allergies, immunizations |
| **Medications** | Drug names, dosages, frequency, refill dates |
| **Appointments** | Date, time, provider name, location, appointment notes |
| **Clinical Notes** | SOAP notes, progress assessments (entered by Physician Users) |
| **Recovery Data** | Post-surgical progress, pain scores, mobility assessments, wound photos |
| **Lifestyle Data** | Diet logs, exercise logs, sleep patterns, snoring detection results |

### 1.3 Device and Usage Information

We automatically collect technical information about your device and how you use the App:

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| **Device Information** | Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers (UDID, IDFA, Android ID) |
| **Log Data** | IP address, browser type, access times, pages viewed, app features used |
| **Location Data** | GPS location (if you enable location services), approximate location from IP address |
| **Performance Data** | Crash logs, error reports, app performance metrics |
| **Analytics Data** | Feature usage, session duration, click/tap events |

### 1.4 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from third parties, including:

- **Healthcare providers**: Clinical data shared with your consent
- **Social media platforms**: If you connect your account (e.g., Google Sign-In)
- **Payment processors**: Transaction confirmations

## 5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

### 5.1 Encryption

- **At rest**: AES-256 encryption for all stored data
- **In transit**: TLS 1.3 for all data transmission
- **Passwords**: Bcrypt hashing with salt

### 5.2 Access Controls

- **Role-based permissions**: Users can only access data they are authorized to view
- **Multi-factor authentication** (MFA): Optional for all users, required for Physician Users
- **Principle of least privilege**: Employees access only data necessary for their job

### 5.3 Monitoring and Auditing

- **Audit logs**: All PHI access is logged (who, when, what) and retained for 7 years (HIPAA)
- **Intrusion detection**: Real-time monitoring for suspicious activity
- **Regular security assessments**: Quarterly vulnerability scans, annual penetration testing

### 5.4 Employee Training

- All employees complete **annual HIPAA training**
- Security awareness training (phishing prevention, password hygiene)
- Confidentiality agreements

### 5.5 Incident Response

In the event of a data breach:
1. **Containment**: Immediately isolate affected systems
2. **Investigation**: Determine scope and cause
3. **Notification**: Notify affected users within **72 hours** (GDPR) or as required by law
4. **Remediation**: Implement fixes to prevent recurrence

**Despite these measures, no system is 100% secure.** You acknowledge that we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your information for different periods depending on the type of data and legal requirements:

### 6.1 Active Accounts

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|------------------|
| **Personal Information** | Duration of account + 30-day grace period after deletion |
| **Health Information** | Duration of account + 30-day grace period after deletion |
| **Audit logs** (PHI access) | 7 years (HIPAA requirement) |
| **Payment records** | 7 years (IRS requirement) |
| **Physician credentials** | Duration of physician account + 3 years |

### 6.2 Deleted Accounts

When you delete your account:
1. **Immediate**: You lose access to the App
2. **30-day grace period**: Data is soft-deleted but recoverable (in case of accidental deletion)
3. **After 30 days**: Data is permanently deleted within **90 days**

**Exceptions** (data NOT deleted):
- Audit logs (7-year retention)
- Anonymized, aggregated data
- Data required for legal/regulatory compliance
- Data subject to ongoing legal holds

### 6.3 Inactive Accounts

If your account is inactive for **3 years**, we will:
- Send email notification asking if you want to keep your account
- If no response within **60 days**, delete your account (with 30-day grace period)

## 7. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you have the following rights:

### 7.1 Right to Access (GDPR Art. 15, CCPA)

**You can view your data at any time:**
- In-app: Navigate to Settings > Privacy > My Data
- Export: Settings > Privacy > Export My Data (JSON format)

**For comprehensive exports** (including physician notes):
- Email: privacy@redpingdoctor.com
- We will respond within **30 days** (GDPR) or **45 days** (CCPA)

### 7.2 Right to Rectification (GDPR Art. 16)

**Correct inaccurate data:**
- In-app: Edit vitals, medications, appointments directly
- For account info: Settings > Profile > Edit
- For physician notes you dispute: Contact the physician or email privacy@redpingdoctor.com (we'll flag the note as "disputed")

### 7.3 Right to Erasure / "Right to Be Forgotten" (GDPR Art. 17, CCPA)

**Delete your account and data:**
- In-app: Settings > Account > Delete Account
- Email: privacy@redpingdoctor.com with subject "Account Deletion Request"

**Timeline**: 30-day grace period + 90 days for permanent deletion

**Exceptions** (data NOT deleted):
- Audit logs (7-year legal requirement)
- Anonymized data
- Legal holds

### 7.4 Right to Data Portability (GDPR Art. 20)

**Export your data in a machine-readable format:**
- **Basic/Professional tiers**: JSON or PDF export
- **Ultra tier**: HL7 FHIR format (for EHR integration)

### 7.5 Right to Object (GDPR Art. 21)

**Opt out of certain data processing:**
- **Marketing emails**: Click "Unsubscribe" in any email or Settings > Notifications > Marketing Emails
- **Analytics**: Settings > Privacy > Usage Analytics > Disable
- **Research**: Settings > Privacy > Anonymized Research > Disable

### 7.6 Right to Restrict Processing (GDPR Art. 18)

**Limit how we process your data:**
- Example: Prevent physician access while disputing accuracy
- Email: privacy@redpingdoctor.com with your request

### 7.7 Right to Non-Discrimination (CCPA)

**We will NOT discriminate against you for exercising your rights:**
- We will not deny services
- We will not charge different prices
- We will not provide different quality of service

### 7.8 Automated Decision-Making (GDPR Art. 22)

**We do NOT make automated decisions with legal or significant effects.**

- The Health Index is for **informational purposes only**
- All clinical decisions are made by **your licensed healthcare provider**
