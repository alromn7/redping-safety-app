## ‚ú® Core Features

### Emergency Response
- **One-Touch SOS**: Press and hold the RedPing button for 10 seconds to activate emergency alert
- **Automatic Crash Detection**: Advanced sensors detect vehicular crashes and automatically trigger SOS
- **Fall Detection**: Identifies serious falls and initiates emergency protocols
- **Real-Time Location Tracking**: Continuous GPS tracking during active SOS sessions
- **Emergency Contact Notifications**: Instantly alerts your designated emergency contacts

### Safety Monitoring
- **24/7 Sensor Monitoring**: Continuous accelerometer and gyroscope monitoring
- **Drowsiness Detection**: AI-powered voice analysis to detect driver fatigue
- **Transportation Mode Detection**: Automatically identifies driving, walking, or stationary states
- **Battery Optimization**: Intelligent power management for extended monitoring

### Search & Rescue (SAR) Integration
- **Professional SAR Dashboard**: Real-time emergency feed for registered SAR organizations
- **Case Management**: Track and coordinate rescue operations
- **Two-Way Communication**: Direct messaging between users and SAR teams
- **Priority-Based Triage**: Automatic risk assessment and priority assignment

### Community Safety
- **Hazard Alerts**: Weather warnings, environmental hazards, and community reports
- **Location Sharing**: Share your real-time location with trusted contacts
- **Group Activities**: Coordinate safety for group adventures and expeditions
- **Family Mode**: Keep track of family members' locations and safety status

### Communication
- **SMS Emergency Escalation**: Automatic SMS notifications every 2 minutes during SOS
- **Voice Verification**: AI voice analysis to prevent false alarms
- **Multi-Channel Notifications**: Email, SMS, and push notifications
- **Emergency Contact Hierarchy**: Primary and secondary contact escalation

### Subscription Tiers
- **Free Tier**: Basic crash detection and manual SOS
- **Pro Tier** ($9.99/month): Advanced AI features and fall detection
- **Ultra Tier** ($19.99/month): Premium safety suite with extended features
- **Family Tier** ($29.99/month): Protection for up to 6 family members

## üèóÔ∏è Technology Stack

### Mobile Application
- **Framework**: Flutter (Dart)
- **Platforms**: Android (iOS coming soon)
- **Architecture**: Clean Architecture with Repository Pattern
- **State Management**: Riverpod

### Sensors & Hardware
- **Accelerometer**: High-frequency motion detection
- **Gyroscope**: Orientation and rotation tracking
- **GPS**: High-accuracy location services (< 10m accuracy)
- **Battery Monitor**: Real-time battery level tracking

### Backend Infrastructure
- **Database**: Firebase Firestore (real-time NoSQL)
- **Authentication**: Firebase Auth with Google Sign-In
- **Cloud Functions**: Node.js serverless functions
- **Storage**: Firebase Cloud Storage
- **Analytics**: Firebase Crashlytics

### AI & Machine Learning
- **Voice Analysis**: ChatGPT API integration for drowsiness detection
- **Pattern Recognition**: Custom algorithms for crash/fall detection
- **Risk Assessment**: AI-powered emergency priority calculation

### Payment Processing
- **Payment Gateway**: Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 compliant)
- **Subscription Management**: Automated billing and renewal
- **Security**: Token-based payments, no card storage
