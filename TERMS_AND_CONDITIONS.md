## 5. Services Provided

### 5.1 General Features
The App provides the following services:

#### For Patient Users:
- **Health tracking**: Log vital signs (blood pressure, heart rate, weight, etc.)
- **Health Index**: Calculate a general wellness score based on user-entered data
- **Medication management**: Track medications and set reminders
- **Appointment scheduling**: Manage medical appointments
- **Recommendations**: Receive general wellness tips and guidance (not medical advice)
- **Doctor Connect**: Link with verified healthcare providers for supervised tracking

#### For Physician Users (Professional/Ultra Tiers):
- **Patient monitoring**: View health data of linked Patient Users
- **Progress notes**: Document clinical observations (SOAP format)
- **Recovery journey tracking**: Collaborate with patients on post-surgical or treatment recovery
- **Alerts**: Receive notifications for critical health values or missed medications
- **Secure messaging**: Communicate with patients (Professional/Ultra tiers)

#### For Organizations (Ultra Tier):
- **Admin dashboard**: Manage multiple patients and care teams
- **Population health analytics**: View aggregated health metrics
- **API access**: Integrate with EHR systems via HL7 FHIR (optional)

### 5.2 Subscription Tiers
Services are provided through tiered subscriptions:
- **Basic**: Self-tracking features
- **Professional**: Adds Doctor Connect and Recovery Journey
- **Ultra**: Adds Admin Management, multi-provider teams, and API access

See Section 6 for pricing and payment terms.
