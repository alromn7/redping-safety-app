## ‚úÖ SIMPLIFIED PRICING - NO CONSUMER ADD-ONS

### What Changed:
**REMOVED:**
- ‚ùå Satellite SOS device (+$3/month)
- ‚ùå Premium analytics (+$2/month)
- ‚ùå Family plan (+$8/month)
- ‚ùå Priority coordination (+$5/month)

### Why Removed:
1. **User confusion** - Multiple price points confuse the value proposition
2. **Looks like coverage tiers** - Creates insurance appearance
3. **Legal risk** - Tiered pricing = tiered coverage in regulator's eyes
4. **Complexity** - Harder to communicate, harder to sell
5. **Support burden** - More SKUs = more support questions

### What We Keep:
‚úÖ **$5/month base** - Simple, clear, no confusion  
‚úÖ **$15/month repayment** - Automatic after using fund  
‚úÖ **B2B enterprise plans** - Separate product line
